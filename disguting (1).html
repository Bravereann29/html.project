In these situations, you might try practicing the following steps of radical acceptance:

  1. Observe the ways in which you may be questioning or fighting against your emotions or reality.
  2. Remind yourself that you cannot change your emotions or reality in this particular situation.
  3. Try to note any causes for the situation at hand. Acknowledge that you may not be able to control how you feel, but you can control how you proceed.
  4. Practice acceptance and use positive self-talk to tell yourself you are willing to accept this situation, even if it is challenging.
  5. List all the behaviors in which you'd partake if you had already accepted this situation. Act this way until you reach true acceptance.
  6. Think of ways to accept the situation if it worsens.
  7. Use mindfulness or meditation to connect with yourself and manage any bodily sensations.
  8. Allow disappointment, sadness, grief, or anger to arise and exist, but do not act on them.
  9. Acknowledge that life can be worth living, even when there is pain.
  10. Create a pros and cons list if you continue to resist acceptance.
